----How to play----

KEYBOARD CONTROLS:
WASD to move.
E to interact.
ESC to pause the game.
SHIFT to sprint. (One use only - use it wisely!)
SPACEBAR to jump.
LEFT CLICK to hack the enemy/hack the panel.
MOUSE  to look around.

CONTROLLER CONTROLS:
LEFT JOYSTICK to move.
LEFT JOYSTICK BUTTON to sprint. (One use only - use it wisely!)
RT to hack the enemy/hack the panel.
DPAD UP to interact.
DPAD DOWN to jump.
RIGHT JOYSTICK to look around.
OPTIONS BUTTON to pause the game.

ICONS:

Green Icon - Undetected/Safe
Red Icon - Detected
Purple Icon - Looking at hackable object/person
Blue Icon - Emergency Sprint Ready

----Release notes----

An enemy faction member stole a data chip from the company you work under. 
You've been hired to get it back.

Sneak around this cyberpunk world, and locate the missing chip. Get it, and get out - without being caught.

-Interact with Hack Points to unlock doors.

-Hack the enemy to stun them momentarily

-Use invisibility spots to hide from the enemy.

Watch your back. Complete your mission.
